The Silver Shield

Lemon Crafty wasn’t like any other mere dads in the town. Surely, he dropped his kids off at school everyday, acted out multiple voices every night for Story Time with his toddlers despite the act didn’t match his face or age at all. But Lamont had secrets—the first being his real name, Jackson Phillips. He is one of the top secret officer of IMC and well-respected by his subordinates, as well as his junior and senior. Thus his title—The Silver Shield—obtained by protecting many of the soldiers with his Silver-alike shield, when the enemy’s ambush has taken into their base.
