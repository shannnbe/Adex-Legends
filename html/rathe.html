Rathe

Interdimensional Fatui

Rathe is a whirlwind fighter, she is able to execute deadly attacks and manipulate her surrounding by opening rifts in the fabric of reality — but everything has a price to pay. Years ago, she woke up in an Alpha A Group (AAG) facility with no memory of who she was. The only human who has ever cared to her was betrayed by her own partner, hence leaving the girl - locked alone on the cold empty room. The tiny and frightened little girl who has no one to rely on, started to listening to the voice in her head, and thus by the voice’s lead, she could break through the prison and escape into - the reality.
