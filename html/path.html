Pathfinder is the picture of optimism, despite his circumstances. A MRVN (Mobile Robotic Versatile eNtity) modified to do task in location scouting and surveying harsh terrain, he booted up no one know-years ago, in an abandoned facility with no idea who created him or why. With only his MRVN designation to hint at his identity, Pathfinder eventually set off in search of his creator and thus he joined the Adex Arena.
